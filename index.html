About Us



Our business was founded by young and dynamic entrepreneur brothers with the goal of making essential medicines available to everyone at an affordable price.


At Rapha Medicals, we offer a staggering 15 to 80% discount on all medicines, making it accessible to every individual, especially monthly prescription customers such as diabetics, BP patients, cardiac patients, and others. Besides medicines, we also offer discounts on cosmetics products.
